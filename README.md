## 🧠 Why I do this

> “If the textbook doesn't teach you, you teach yourself — and then you share it.”

Official IGCSE textbooks (CUP, Hodder) often skip crucial explanations, misorder topics, and even contain arithmetic errors (e.g. `8/10 = 0.4`). I've been fixing these gaps one by one. These repos are the result.

If you find them useful, **star ⭐ them** — it helps others find them too.
